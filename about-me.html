I have lived in Ohio my entire life, and I like it here. I like to explore other areas too, but the there's something about the seasonal Midwest atmosphere that keeps me close to home. I just can't stay out of The Shire for too long.

I have always been interested in technology; it's why I started my academic career in engineering. Using technology can be frustrating and unintuitive at times, but I think technology can be harnessed in ways that improve the human experience and make our lives better. I think it's important that technology is used and created ethically as well, and I have dedicated some of my time to discussing ethical questions relating to technology in the Applied Ethics and Technology club at Ohio State.

I also love space. I think the future of humanity depends on our ability to venture out into the stars, and it's a very exciting time in the aerospace industry right now. This passion for space exploration is why I chose to study aerospace engineering early on in college. I actually switched from mechanical engineering, which upon reflection probably would have been a better choice. I don't mind though. I think following your heart can take you to a good place in the end.

I have a strong passion for the arts. I did choir and was part of an acapella group in high school. I even did show choir one year, but I'm definitely not a dancer. I have always been shy, but there's something about performing music that brings me out of my shell. I also like to compose music as a hobby. I'm not great at it, but you can check out some samples of my work in the music page. I also view video games as the ultimate artistic medium. There's no other type of art that can immerse you in another world as much as a video game, and as virtual reality becomes more immersive the line between reality and the virtual world will continue to blur. Some of my all-time favorite games are Final Fantasy 7, Super Mario Galaxy, and Everhood. I also really like movies, and I used to be really into special effects, which is how I got into video editing. I also made some props as a child, and I've attached a picture of a lightsaber that I built when I was younger. My favorite movie is The Matrix.

I like helping people and playing support. When I was involved in Sigma Gamma Tau during my time as an aerospace engineering major, I got the opportunity to help with some of the review sessions. These sessions were created so that more experienced students could help students in lower-level aerospace classes do better. It was also an opportunity to reflect on material learned earlier and to improve my own knowledge.

I'm currently on a break from school, but when I return, I would like to do some courses in philosophy and architecture. I'm naturally curious about a lot of things, and I think it's good to take some time to explore different ideas and broaden my skillset.

Thank you for taking the time to read this, I hope I was able to express what I'm about. If you want to talk business, or whatever, you can contact me at felixjanow@outlook.com and janow.6@osu.edu.
